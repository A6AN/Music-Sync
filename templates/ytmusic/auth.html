Connect YouTube Music

Step 1: Get Your Headers
  1. Open YouTube Music in Firefox (recommended)
  2. Make sure you're logged in to your account
  3. Press F12 to open Developer Tools
  4. Go to the Network tab
  5. Filter by /browse
  6. Click on any request that appears
  7. In the request details, find Request Headers
  8. Click the RAW toggle button to view raw headers
  9. Right-click and Select All, then copy
Important: Your headers contain authentication cookies. Keep them secure and don't share them with anyone!
